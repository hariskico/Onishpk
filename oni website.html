Company

Three decades of healthcare innovation

Albania's only company to have built, deployed, and maintained large-scale HIS/HMS platforms across university and regional hospitals.

Who We Are

ONI shpk is an Albanian technology company established in 1994, specializing in Healthcare Information Systems, enterprise ERP solutions, and digital transformation for public institutions. Our platforms SIIS and SISP are the only Albanian-developed HIS/HMS solutions certified with national intellectual property rights (RDA 50, RDA 48/3) — deployed in the country's most critical healthcare environments.

We operate across Albania and Kosovo, providing mission-critical digital infrastructure, 24/7 technical maintenance, and continuous system development to university hospitals, regional hospitals, private clinics, and primary care networks.

/ Arkimedo 21
ERP 360°

Enterprise Resource Planning
for public institutions

Arkimedo 21 unifies asset management, logistics, finance, HR, GIS/BIM, and energy monitoring in a single web-based platform — fully traceable, real-time, and configured to the Albanian institutional and regulatory context.

Deployed Capabilities
Barcode-verified interventions
Every technical intervention carries a unique barcode. Completion is verified within the system before the intervention is closed.
Smart notification system
Real-time alerts via WhatsApp and system notifications for defects, in-progress interventions, and operational status changes.
HVAC monitoring
Live monitoring of heating and cooling infrastructure across all buildings managed by the Municipality of Tirana.
HIS integration
Seamless data bridge between Arkimedo 21 and SIIS/SISP platforms for unified asset and pharmaceutical logistics.
