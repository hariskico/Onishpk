About Us

Three Decades of Healthcare Innovation

Founded in 1994, ONI shpk is Albania's only company that has successfully developed and deployed large-scale HIS/HMS platforms in university hospitals, regional hospitals, and primary care networks.

🎯

Mission

To modernize healthcare through secure, reliable digital solutions that improve patient care, transparency, and operational efficiency.

🌍

Vision

Security

Partnership

Our Journey
